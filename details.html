CANADA JOBS RECRUITERS

Job Details

Requirements:

  • Relevant Hospitality qualifications.
  • Extensive restaurant industry experience (Essential).
  • Minimum 5-8 years experience in a high-end restaurant & 5+ years in F&B management (Essential).
  • Not negotiable: 4+ years as a Restaurant General Manager.
  • Experience in 4/5 star restaurants.
  • Proficiency in MS Office (Word, Excel, Outlook).

  • Ensure proper HR management for all employees.
  • Monitor all operational processes & ensure excellence.
